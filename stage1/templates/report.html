DQAR Methodology. This report was generated by Sonian's Digital Quality Audit Readiness (DQAR) Stage 1 conformance kit. Stage 1 tests five independent dimensions: Bulk FHIR API server conformance (Stage 1a-I), Bulk FHIR extract packaging (Stage 1a-II), NDJSON structural validity (Stage 1b), and FHIR R4 + US Core 6.1.0 profile conformance (Stage 1c-I / 1c-II). A plan may pass 1a-I and 1b while every resource fails 1c — these are independent questions.

Three-tier findings. Tier 1 (Governance Gap) identifies organizational failures that will recur next measurement period without process change. Tier 2 (Measure Data Gap) identifies technical data quality failures that affect quality measure rates today. Tier 3 (Digital Readiness Gap) identifies capability gaps against MY2029 ECDS mandatory reporting and the CMS-0057-F January 1, 2027 deadline.

Conformance target: US Core 6.1.0 (USCDI v3). Plans on US Core 3.1.1 should declare their version; Sonian assesses against the available version and documents the conformance delta as a Tier 3 finding.
